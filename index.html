About our department head

The Head Faculties of SMIC play a vital role in shaping the academic excellence and overall environment of the institution. With years of experience and deep expertise in their respective fields, they not only deliver knowledge but also ignite a passion for learning among students. Their leadership ensures that the college stays committed to innovation, discipline, and high educational standards. They actively mentor students, helping them in academic pursuits, career planning, and personal growth. By fostering a culture of curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking, the Head Faculties help students become future leaders and responsible citizens. They encourage research, participation in various activities, and the pursuit of excellence both inside and outside the classroom. Their vision and dedication continue to drive SMIC towards becoming one of the most respected institutions in the region. Through their hard work and commitment, the Head Faculties inspire both students and fellow educators to achieve greater heights every day.
